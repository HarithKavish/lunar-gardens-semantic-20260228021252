How Lunar Gardens Work

Closed-Loop Systems

Lunar gardens use hydroponics and aeroponics to grow plants without soil, recycling water and nutrients to minimize waste.

Radiation Protection

Plants are grown in domed structures with radiation shielding to protect both crops and astronauts from lunar radiation.

Automation & AI

Advanced sensors and AI monitor soil moisture, temperature, and nutrient levels to optimize plant growth in real-time.

Our Projects

Moon Base Alpha

A prototype lunar greenhouse designed to support a small colony of astronauts, featuring vertical farming techniques.

Martian Terraforming

Research into growing hardy plant species on Mars, including algae and genetically modified crops, to establish a sustainable ecosystem.
